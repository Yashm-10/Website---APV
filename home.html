About Us:

We at Aditya Pure Veg Hotel, Pune have a tradition of 11 years focusing on offering a wide variety of delicious vegetarian dishes in a clean and welcoming environment. We aim to cater to a wide palate by including North Indian, South Indian and Indo-Chinese cuisines with options for A la carte, meals and desserts. We prioritise hygiene, affordability and quick service.

More than Just a Meal

  • Ambience: We have created a pleasant and inviting atmosphere with comfortable seating and good lighting.
  • Friendly Staff: We provide friendly and efficient service to ensure a positive experience for customers.
  • Affordability: We offer reasonable prices for the quality and variety of food provided.
  • Quick service: Our service is efficient, especially for fast-casual options.
